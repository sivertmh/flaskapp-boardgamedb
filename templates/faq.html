Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, go to the login page and follow the link to sign up.
To be more specific, click "Log In" in the navigational bar, then find the link in the text "Don't have a user? You can sign up here." and enter your details to create your account.

How do I log in?

After having created a account, you can log in. Go to the login-page and enter required details. If you have deactivated your account, you will not be able to log in.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you will find this option on your account page. Near the bottom of the page, you should be able to find the "Danger Zone". To delete your account, type in the username and password first. Upon clicking the "Delete account" button, you will be asked to confirm the deletion.
